About The Talent Hunt

India's First Global Sales Talent Hunt is a groundbreaking initiative designed to identify, nurture, and launch the next generation of world-class sales professionals. This multi-city talent hunt brings together ambitious candidates from across India for interactive challenges, mentorship from global sales leaders, and live evaluation rounds — giving you a direct launchpad to an international sales career. Whether you're a seasoned closer or a hungry newcomer, this is your moment to shine on the global stage.

Who Can Apply?

We're looking for driven individuals ready to make an impact. Check if you qualify below.

Sales Professionals

Experienced B2B or B2C sales professionals with a proven track record of meeting or exceeding targets. Minimum 1 year of relevant experience.

Fresh Graduates

Ambitious graduates from any discipline who are passionate about building a career in sales. Strong communication skills are a must.

Career Changers

Professionals from any field looking to pivot into a dynamic sales career. We value diverse perspectives and transferable skills.

Your Path To Getting Selected

From the moment you register, you'll experience a transparent and merit-driven selection process. At every stage, you'll have the chance to demonstrate your potential, engage with global sales leaders, and discover what makes this talent hunt unlike any other.

1

Registration

Submit your profile through our portal. Tell us about your sales ambitions and why you want to compete on the global stage.

2

Screening
